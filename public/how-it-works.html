How it works

30 seconds.
Years of guesswork gone.

One selfie and a quick shelf scan is all it takes. Skin AI reads your skin at a clinical level and builds a personalized routine that actually fits you — not a quiz template.

01

Scan your face

Take a selfie in natural light. That's it. Skin AI's computer vision model reads 14 distinct skin markers from a single image — no dermatologist, no lab, no waiting room. We look at:

  • Hydration levels and barrier integrity
  • Oil distribution and pore visibility
  • Texture, tone uniformity, and pigmentation
  • Fine lines, redness, sensitivity zones
  • Under-eye health and sun damage indicators

The result is a detailed skin profile — your actual skin type, not the one you selected from a dropdown in 2019.

02

Scan your shelf

Photograph the products you already own. Skin AI reads every ingredient list using our database of over 14,000 cosmetic ingredients. We then run a full conflict analysis across everything on your shelf:

  • Ingredients that cancel each other's effects
  • Products that over-exfoliate or over-stimulate
  • pH incompatibilities you'd never notice
  • Ingredients your skin type specifically reacts to
  • Gaps in your routine you didn't know existed

Most beta users had at least one harmful conflict in their existing routine. Many had three or four.

03

Get your routine

We output a morning and evening routine, step by step, in correct application order. Each step includes notes on timing, layering logic, and why that product is (or isn't) right for you.

  • Precise application sequence — 7 seconds vs. 30 minutes actually matters
  • Seasonal recalibration as your skin's needs shift
  • Product swap suggestions within your budget
  • Evidence-based recommendations, not influencer picks

Your routine updates automatically as your skin changes. One scan every few weeks keeps everything current.
