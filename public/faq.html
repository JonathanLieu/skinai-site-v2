The product

You take a selfie in natural light. Our computer vision model processes the image to detect 14 distinct skin markers — hydration levels, oil distribution, texture, pore size, redness, pigmentation, fine lines, barrier health, and more. The analysis runs locally on our servers and takes under 10 seconds. No special equipment, no app download required at launch.

For the shelf scan, you photograph your products and we read the ingredient lists automatically using OCR and our ingredient database. You can also type in product names directly if you prefer. Either way, we cross-reference everything against our database of 14,000+ cosmetic ingredients.

Our beta testing shows strong agreement with dermatologist assessments for the 14 markers we measure. Skin analysis accuracy depends heavily on photo quality — good natural lighting makes a significant difference. We built an in-app guide to help you get a consistently accurate image every time.

We recommend a full rescan every 4–6 weeks, or whenever you notice a significant change in your skin (seasonal shift, stress, hormonal change, etc.). Premium members get automatic reminders calibrated to their scan history and routine complexity.

Privacy & data
