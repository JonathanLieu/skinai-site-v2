Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026 · Effective: March 18, 2026

Skin AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have over it. We've written this to be readable — not to obscure things in legal language.

What we collect

Information you provide directly

  • Email address — when you join the waitlist or create an account.
  • Name — optional, provided at signup.
  • Payment information — processed directly by Stripe. We never see or store your full card number.
  • Messages — when you contact us via email or the contact form.

Information we collect automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, time spent. Used to improve the product.
  • Device information — browser type, operating system, screen size. Used for compatibility.
  • IP address — used for security, fraud prevention, and approximate geolocation (country level only).
  • Cookies — see the Cookies section below.

Skin data and photos

When you use Skin AI's analysis features:

  • Photos are transmitted securely to our servers for processing, then deleted by default within 24 hours. We do not retain images unless you explicitly enable the progress photo tracking feature.
  • Skin marker data (the 14 measurements derived from your photo) is stored in your account to power your routine and history. You can delete this at any time.
  • Product data (your scanned products and ingredient lists) is stored to generate and update your routine.

How we use your data

  • To provide, operate, and improve Skin AI's features.
  • To send you your waitlist confirmation and pre-launch updates (you can unsubscribe at any time).
  • To process payments and manage your subscription via Stripe.
  • To respond to your support requests and messages.
  • To monitor service security and prevent fraud or abuse.
  • To understand how the product is used, in aggregate and anonymized form.

We do not use your data to train third-party AI models, sell to advertisers, or share with cosmetic brands for marketing purposes.

Data sharing

We share data only with the following categories of service providers, under strict data processing agreements:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Supabase — database hosting
  • Netlify — web hosting and CDN
  • Transactional email provider — for sending account-related emails

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party. Ever.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Session cookies — to keep you logged in
  • Analytics cookies — anonymous, aggregate usage data only (no cross-site tracking)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. The site will continue to function, though some features may behave differently.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, reach out via our contact form with "Privacy Request" in your message. We'll respond within 30 days.

Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we purge all personal data within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregate data (no personal identifiers) may be retained indefinitely for product improvement.

Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3) for all data in transit. Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256. We conduct regular security reviews. Despite these measures, no system is perfectly secure — please use a strong, unique password for your account.

Children's privacy

Skin AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

Changes to this policy

We'll notify you by email and update the "Last updated" date above if we make material changes. Continued use of Skin AI after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about this policy? Use our contact form.
